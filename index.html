About Me

Hello, I am Arunkumar

Full Stack Developer

I am Arunkumar Dhanraj, from Guduvancheri, Chengalpattu. I completed my Diploma in Chemical Engineering at the Institute of Chemical Technology in Chennai.

After completing my diploma, I worked in various roles at different companies. However, I found these jobs uninteresting and they did not provide me with a satisfactory income. It was during this time that my school teacher introduced me to the Freshworks STS Software Academy.

Intrigued by the program, I decided to apply, and fortunately, I was selected. Being part of the academy is presenting a fantastic chance for me to further my career aspirations. I created an application ‘Creckett’, using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Servlet, JSP, Core Java, and MySQL.

Besides my academic and professional pursuits, I enjoy playing cricket, and watching movies. I aspire to become a Full Stack Developer. I believe that becoming a Full Stack Developer will allow me to achieve in my life.
